Skin Defender

Keep your skin safe

Most people at risk for skin cancer only see their dermatologist once or twice per year. With Skin Defender, use your iPhone to track skin changes between appointments so clinicians have more information to guide treatment. Evolution, or changes within moles and other lesions, is a critical piece of information for dermatologists. Sharing a slideshow of a skin area since your last appointment will help your doctor make an accurate diagnosis and select the right treatment without waiting for additional tests. Take control of your care. Ensure you receive the treatment you need. It could save your life. Download Skin Defender for iPhone today.
